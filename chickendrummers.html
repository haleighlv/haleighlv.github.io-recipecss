Crunchy Chicken Drummers

Serves 4, prep time 0:10, cook time 0:35

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs butter
  • 90g brown rice crackers
  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 egg (lightly beaten)

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C (180C fan-forced).
  2. Line a roasting tin with foil and brush with the melted butter.
  3. Place the brown rice crackers in a zip-lock bag. Close the bag and crush the contents to find crumbs with a rolling pin.
  4. Dip one or two drumsticks at a time in the egg, then add to the zip-lock bag and shake to coat the chicken evenly.
  5. Transfer to the prepared roasting tin.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the coating is browned and crispy.
