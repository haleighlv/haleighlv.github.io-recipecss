Sausage Rolls

Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1kg sausage mince
  • 6 sheets of puff pastry
  • 1 packet of Continental Chicken Noodle Soup Mix
  • 1 egg (lightly beaten, for brushing pastry)

Method

  1. Mix the mince and soup mix together.
  2. Place a long 'sausage like' amount down the centre of 1/2 a sheet of pastry.
  3. Roll into a sausage roll and press pastry edges together. Cut into desired lengths.
  4. Place on oven tray and brush pastry with egg. Bake at 220ÂºC until pastry is crispy.
