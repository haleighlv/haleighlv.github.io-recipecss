Apricot Chicken

Serves 6, prep time 0:10, cook time 0:30

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken thigh fillets, skin removed and diced into large pieces
  • 3 tbs plain flour
  • 2 x 40g French Onion dried soup mix sachets
  • 1 litre apricot nectar

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Toss diced chicken in flour to coat.
  3. Mix soup mix satchels with the apricot nectar.
  4. Place chicken in an ovenproof dish and pour over the nectar mix. Place lid on the dish to cook. Bake for 45 mins.
